About the Game


Choice is a unique and immersive 2D text-based adventure game that generates a new and exciting story each time you play. Leveraging advanced AI technologies, the game offers an unparalleled interactive experience where the narrative and visuals are dynamically created based on your choices. Drawing inspiration from historical events and famous literary works that you choose, Choice aims to provide an educational and empathetic gaming experience.


By weaving elements from these rich sources into the game, players can enjoy stories that are not only entertaining but also enriching and thought-provoking. This project is a tribute to the timeless art of storytelling and its power to educate and inspire. Through engaging narratives, Choice encourages players to explore different perspectives, fostering a deeper understanding of diverse cultures, eras, and philosophies. The dynamic interplay of player decisions and AI-generated content ensures that no two playthroughs are the same, offering endless replayability and a continually evolving adventure.

Features


  • Dynamic Story Generation: Each game session is driven by the OpenAI API, ensuring that no two adventures are the same. The AI crafts unique and compelling stories based on player inputs, drawing inspiration from historical events and famous literary works. This results in narratives that are not only engaging but also educational and thought-provoking, fostering a deeper understanding of diverse cultures, eras, and philosophies.

  • Visual Storytelling: Enhance your adventure with custom images generated by the DALLE-3 API. Visuals are created in real-time to match the unfolding narrative, providing a rich and immersive experience that complements the text-based gameplay. These dynamically generated images bring the stories to life, adding a visual dimension that enhances the overall storytelling experience.

  • Cross-Platform Availability: Built to run on Windows, macOS, and Linux, Choice offers a native-like experience on all major operating systems. This ensures that players can enjoy the game on their preferred platform without any compromise in performance or functionality, making it accessible to a wide audience.

  • Intuitive Interface: The game features a simple yet engaging user interface built with the Electron framework using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. This intuitive design ensures an accessible and enjoyable experience for all players, allowing them to easily navigate through the game and focus on the unfolding story. The interface is crafted to be user-friendly, making the game approachable for both new and experienced gamers alike.
